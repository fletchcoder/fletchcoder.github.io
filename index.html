About Me

My name is Drake and I've had a passion for computers for as long as I can remember. I played many Adobe Flash games and took typing courses in school as a kid, which both helped to develop my interest in computer technology. As I grew older, I became more interested in the study of computer science and went to college to pursue it as a major.

I started by learning C++ and writing beginner programs in my college classes. I quickly learned that back-end programming was not where my passion was and switched to front-end. HTML, CSS, and JavaScript came to me much more naturally. After I took a course in Photoshop, I felt that my designs would truly demonstrate my creative potential.
